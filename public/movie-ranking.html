The Best Movies According to Leonardo

My favorite superhero movies of all time.

The Avengers

An epic superhero movie that brings multiple heroes together to save the world.

The Dark Knight

A darker and more mature Batman movie with an unforgettable villain.

Captain America: The First Avenger

A great origin story with a strong sense of heroism and sacrifice.

Iron Man

The movie that started the MCU, full of charisma and technology.

Avengers: Infinity War

High stakes, emotional moments, and an incredible crossover of heroes.
